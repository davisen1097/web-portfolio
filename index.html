Ten Musical Group is a Mauritian local Band formed in 1997 which offers a wide variety of choices of entertainment for your event. Ten Musical Group was named by the boss whose nickname is "Ten Mooken". Moreover, we have recorded around 25 album with several well known artist which was sold both on local and international markets. Ten Musical Group is a well known band in its country, mostly for the sega songs. Beyond the sega, the band plays all genres of music. In addition, the Mauritian tamil community recognise our band as the best performance live group for entertaining special cultural events with tamil and sega tamil music.
